About Me

I am currently employed as a Research Assistant at Professor Mark Liao's Multimedia Laboratory, housed within the Institute of Information Science (IIS) at Academia Sinica. I received my M.S. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering and a B.S. degree in Computer Information and Network Engineering from Lunghwa University of Science and Technology. My primary research interest is centered on leveraging advancements in image processing, computer vision, and machine learning to address real-world challenges. I have a particular focus on object detection and tracking, areas where I have made significant strides with my project NeighborTrack. Notably, NeighborTrack stands as a state-of-the-art contribution to the current UAV123 drone object tracking dataset. For more detailed information about my professional and academic background, I invite you to review my full CV. I am open to further discussions about my research; feel free to reach out to me at franktpmvu@gmail.com.
